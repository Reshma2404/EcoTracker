About the website

What is EcoTrack?

EcoTrack is a smart waste management system that lets citizens report public waste with images, location, and descriptions—so authorities can act faster.

Why it is needed?

Traditional reporting is slow and manual. EcoTrack streamlines reporting, tracking, and cleanup actions—reducing delays and improving accountability.

Advantages of EcoTrack

  • Promotes public participation in cleanliness
  • Reduces environmental pollution
  • Helps authorities manage waste efficiently
  • Creates environmental responsibility and awareness
