About EcoTrack

Transforming Waste Management Through Technology

What is EcoTrack?

EcoTrack is a comprehensive smart waste management system designed to revolutionize how cities handle waste collection and disposal. Our platform bridges the gap between citizens and waste management authorities using cutting-edge technology.

With EcoTrack, users can easily report waste-related issues, track their status in real-time, and contribute to making their communities cleaner and more sustainable. Our admin dashboard provides city officials with complete visibility and control over waste management operations.

Our Mission

To create a cleaner, healthier environment by enabling efficient waste management through community participation and smart technology. We aim to reduce pollution, promote recycling, and improve quality of life in urban areas.

Our Vision

A world where waste is managed responsibly and sustainably. We envision smart cities where citizens and authorities work together seamlessly to maintain clean, green communities for future generations.
